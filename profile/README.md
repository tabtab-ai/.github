## TabTabAI

**TabTabAI** builds AI-native data analysis agents that make working with data radically more accessible.

Our mission is to **lower the barrier to getting and using data with AI**, so that anyone – not just data experts – can ask questions, explore insights, and make decisions based on data.  
In short: **data for everyone, not just the few.**

👉 Official website: [https://www.tabtabai.com](https://www.tabtabai.com)

---

### What we do

- **Data Analysis Agents**  
  We design agents that can connect to data sources, understand business questions, and return clear, actionable answers – not just dashboards.

- **Natural Language to Data**  
  Users ask questions in everyday language. Our agents handle the rest: query generation, data fetching, validation, and explanation.

- **Data Democratization**  
  We focus on removing the friction between people and data: no steep learning curves, no complex tools, just understandable insights.

---

### How our agents help

- **For non-technical teams**: Self-serve analytics without learning SQL, BI tools, or data modeling.  
- **For data teams**: Fewer ad-hoc requests; more time for high-leverage work like modeling, experimentation, and strategy.  
- **For leadership**: Faster, clearer visibility into the health of the business.

### Open source at TabTabAI

This GitHub organization hosts our open-source work around data-centric AI agents:

- **Agent skills and connectors** – building blocks to connect to databases, warehouses, and APIs.
- **Query & reasoning engines** – logic for translating natural language into reliable data operations.
- **Example workflows** – reference implementations for analytics, reporting, and decision support.

We welcome:

- **Issues** – bug reports, feature requests, and ideas.
- **Pull Requests** – improvements to code, docs, and examples.
- **Discussions** – questions about architecture, integrations, and real-world use cases.
